The History Of Moxibustion

Moxibustion is one of the oldest healing practices in the world, with a history of over 2,500 years in China. It involves burning dried mugwort (moxa) near the skin to create a gentle, warming sensation.

In traditional Chinese culture, moxibustion is believed to help balance the body's energy and promote a sense of well-being. It has been practiced for generations as part of a holistic approach to health and wellness.

Today, moxibustion is enjoyed by people all over the world as a relaxing and soothing self-care practice. This modern smokeless design makes it easier than ever to experience this ancient tradition in your own home.
