Weighted Blanket Buying Guide

Everything you need to know before buying a weighted blanket

⚖️

Choose the Right Weight

Select a blanket that's 10% of your body weight for optimal deep pressure therapy.

🌡️

Consider Temperature

Look for cooling materials like bamboo or moisture-wicking fabrics if you tend to sleep hot.

🧵

Material Matters

Choose soft, durable fabrics like cotton, minky, or fleece based on your preference.

👪

Size Considerations

Match the blanket size to your bed size for maximum coverage and comfort.
