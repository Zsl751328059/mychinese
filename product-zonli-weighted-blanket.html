Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before buying

How do I choose the right weight?

+

Choose 8-10% of your body weight. For example, the 20lb blanket is perfect for adults weighing 180-220 lbs. If it's your first time, allow 7-10 days to adjust to the sensation.

Can I wash this blanket?

+

Yes, it's machine washable, but due to its weight, it may exceed the load capacity of household washing machines. We recommend using a ZONLI duvet cover for easy cleaning or a commercial washer for the blanket itself.

Does it come with a duvet cover?

+

No, the blanket comes alone, but ZONLI duvet covers are available for purchase separately. We highly recommend getting one to make cleaning easier and extend the life of your blanket.

Why is it smaller than a regular comforter?

+

Weighted blankets are intentionally smaller to concentrate the weight directly on your body rather than hanging off the edges. This design maximizes the therapeutic deep pressure effect.

Is this blanket hot?

+

No! The temperature-regulating design provides adaptable warmth in winter and cooling in summer. The breathable polyester fabric works well year-round for most people.

What's special about the 3.0 version?

+

The upgraded 3.0 version features 5"x5" compartments with precision stitching and a 5+2 layer lining. This prevents bead leakage and shifting, ensuring even weight distribution and long-lasting comfort.

✈️ Is this blanket good for travel?

+

Absolutely! This is one of the best travel-weighted blankets available. The 60"x80" size is compact enough for airplanes, road trips, and camping, while the leak-proof 5+2 layer design and 5"x5" compartments ensure beads stay in place even when packed tight.
