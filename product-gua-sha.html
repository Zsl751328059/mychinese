How To Use Your GuaSha Tool

Using your guasha tool is simple and takes just 5-10 minutes a day. Follow these tips for the best experience:

How To Use:

  1. Prepare your skin - Apply facial oil, serum or moisturizer to clean skin
  2. Neck area - Gently glide upward from collarbone to jawline
  3. Jawline & cheeks - Massage from center of face outward
  4. Forehead - Glide upward from eyebrows to hairline
  5. Under eyes - Use gentle pressure from inner corner outward

Care Instructions:

  1. Clean after each use - Wipe with a soft, damp cloth
  2. Store properly - Keep in a cool, dry place
  3. Avoid dropping - Natural stone can break if dropped
  4. Handle gently - Treat your guasha tool with care
