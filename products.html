Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our wellness products

A weighted blanket is a therapeutic blanket filled with materials like glass beads or plastic pellets to provide deep pressure stimulation, which can help reduce anxiety and improve sleep quality by mimicking the feeling of being hugged.

As a general rule, choose a blanket that weighs 10% of your body weight. For example, if you weigh 150 lbs, a 15 lb blanket would be ideal. For children, consult specific weight guidelines for their age and size.

Yes, weighted blankets can be safe for children aged 3 and above when used properly. Always follow weight guidelines (5-10% of body weight) and supervise younger children during use.
