FAQs About Gua Sha

Gua sha is a traditional Chinese therapy that involves scraping the skin with a smooth tool to promote circulation, reduce muscle tension, and improve overall well-being.

Apply facial oil or serum to your skin, then gently glide the gua sha tool in upward and outward motions. Focus on areas like the cheeks, jawline, and forehead.

For best results, use gua sha 2-3 times per week. Listen to your skin and adjust frequency as needed.

Gua sha can help reduce puffiness, improve blood circulation, promote lymphatic drainage, and enhance the absorption of skincare products.
