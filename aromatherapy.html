FAQs About Aromatherapy

Aromatherapy is the practice of using essential oils for therapeutic purposes. The scent molecules interact with your brain to promote relaxation, improve mood, and support overall well-being.

Always dilute essential oils with a carrier oil before applying to skin. Keep them away from eyes and mucous membranes, and never ingest essential oils.

Lavender, chamomile, sandalwood, and ylang-ylang are excellent choices for promoting relaxation and improving sleep quality.

Some essential oils are toxic to pets, especially cats. Avoid using tea tree, citrus, and pine oils around animals, and always consult your vet if unsure.
