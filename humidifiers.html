Humidifier Buying Guide

Everything you need to know before buying a humidifier

🏠

Room Size

Choose a humidifier based on your room size. Small rooms need 1-2L tanks, while large rooms require 4L+ capacity.

❄️

Cool vs Warm Mist

Cool mist is better for summer and allergies. Warm mist provides soothing relief for colds and dry throats.

🔊

Noise Level

For bedrooms, look for quiet operation (under 30dB). Ultrasonic humidifiers are generally quieter than evaporative ones.

⚡

Smart Features

Smart humidifiers offer app control, voice commands, and automatic humidity monitoring for hands-free operation.
