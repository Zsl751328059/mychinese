How To Use And Care For Your Ox Horn Comb

Using an ox horn comb is simple, but proper care will ensure it lasts for many years. Follow these tips for the best experience:

How To Use

  1. Start at the ends - Always comb from the ends of your hair upward to avoid tangles
  2. Use gentle strokes - Let the weight of the comb do the work, don't pull or tug
  3. Massage your scalp - Use the tips of the teeth to gently massage your scalp in circular motions
  4. Comb daily - Use your comb morning and night as part of your hair care routine

How To Care

  1. Clean regularly - Wipe with a soft, damp cloth after each use
  2. Avoid water - Don't soak your comb in water, as this can damage the horn
  3. Keep away from heat - Don't leave your comb in direct sunlight or near heat sources
  4. Oil occasionally - Apply a small amount of mineral oil once a month to keep the horn moisturized

The History Of Chinese Ox Horn Combs

Ox horn combs have been used in China for over 2,000 years. They were once considered luxury items reserved for emperors and nobles, prized for their beauty and durability.

Traditional Chinese craftsmen believe that ox horn has natural properties that make it the perfect material for combs. It's naturally anti-static, smooth, and gentle on both hair and scalp.

Today, the art of making ox horn combs is still passed down from generation to generation. Each comb takes several days to complete, from selecting the raw horn to the final polishing.
