Frequently Asked Questions

About Zen Well Body

What is Zen Well Body?

Zen Well Body is a wellness review website dedicated to helping people find high-quality traditional Chinese wellness products. We research, test, and review products like jade gua sha stones, herbal teas, ox horn combs, moxibustion boxes, and herbal sachets to help you make informed purchasing decisions.

How do you select products to review?

We carefully select products based on their quality, customer reviews, value for money, and alignment with traditional wellness practices. We purchase and test products ourselves whenever possible, and we analyze thousands of customer reviews to identify the best options for our readers.

Is Zen Well Body a store?

No, we are not an online store. We are a review and recommendation website. When we recommend products, we direct you to purchase them from trusted retailers like Amazon, where you can benefit from their buyer protection and fast shipping.

Affiliate & Compensation

Do you earn money from product recommendations?

Yes, we participate in affiliate marketing programs, including the Amazon Associates Program. When you purchase products through our links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep our website running and continue to provide free, valuable content. Read our full Affiliate Disclaimer

Does affiliate compensation affect your reviews?

No. Our editorial content is completely independent from our affiliate relationships. We only recommend products we genuinely believe in and would use ourselves. Our reviews and recommendations are based on thorough research and real-world testing, not on how much commission we might earn.

Products & Recommendations

Are your product recommendations based on testing?

Whenever possible, we purchase and personally test products to provide firsthand experience. For products we haven't tested directly, we base our recommendations on extensive research, ingredient analysis, customer reviews, and comparison with similar products.

How often do you update your reviews?

We regularly review and update our content to ensure accuracy. Product prices, availability, and customer reviews change frequently, so we revisit our key product pages at least quarterly and make updates as needed.

Can I trust your product recommendations?

We take trust seriously. We only recommend products that meet our high standards for quality, value, and authenticity. We clearly indicate when we haven't personally tested a product and always encourage readers to do their own research before making purchase decisions.

Orders & Shipping

I purchased a product through your link - where's my order?

Since we don't sell products directly, we don't handle orders or shipping. Your purchase is made through the retailer's website (like Amazon). Please check your order confirmation email for tracking information, or contact the retailer directly for order-related questions.

The product I bought isn't what I expected. Can you help?

For issues with your order, please contact the retailer where you made the purchase. Most retailers like Amazon have generous return policies and customer service teams that can help resolve issues with orders, returns, or refunds.

Website & Content

Is the information on your website medical advice?

No. The content on Zen Well Body is for informational purposes only and should not be considered medical advice. Our wellness products are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Please consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice.

How can I get in touch with you?

We love hearing from our readers! You can contact us through our Contact Page. Whether you have questions, feedback, or product suggestions, we're here to help.

Can I republish content from your website?

Our content is protected by copyright. If you'd like to share our content, please contact us for permission. We generally allow sharing of our content for non-commercial purposes with proper attribution and a link to the original source.
