Frequently Asked Questions

Are these essential oils 100% pure? +
Yes! All our essential oils are 100% pure and therapeutic grade. They are free from additives, fillers, and synthetic fragrances.
How many oils come in the set? +
Our premium set includes 14 essential oils: lavender, tea tree, eucalyptus, peppermint, lemon, orange, frankincense, rosemary, chamomile, sandalwood, cedarwood, bergamot, ylang-ylang, and patchouli.
Can I use these in a diffuser? +
Absolutely! These oils are perfect for use in ultrasonic diffusers, nebulizers, and humidifiers. Simply add a few drops to water and enjoy the aromatic benefits.
Are these safe for skin use? +
Yes, but always dilute with a carrier oil (like jojoba or coconut oil) before applying to skin. Never apply undiluted essential oils directly.
How should I store essential oils? +
Store in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and heat. Keep the bottles tightly closed when not in use to preserve the oils' potency.
Do these oils expire? +
Essential oils do have a shelf life. Most oils last 1-3 years when stored properly. Citrus oils tend to have shorter shelf lives (6-12 months), while woody oils can last 3-5 years.
