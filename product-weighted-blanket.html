Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about weighted blankets

What size weighted blanket should I get?

+

The general rule is to choose a heavy blanket that's about 10% of your body weight. For example, if you weigh 150 lbs, a 15 lb weighted blanket would be ideal. That said, personal preference matters—if you like more or less pressure, adjust by 1-2 lbs. The blanket should cover your body but doesn't need to hang over the bed edges like a regular comforter.

Can I use a weighted blanket year-round?

+

Yes! Most people can use this weighted blanket year-round. The microfiber material and glass beads help regulate temperature better than traditional heavy blankets. That said, during hot summer months or in warm climates, you might want a lighter weight or use it with AC running. If you tend to overheat easily, check out our YnM Cooling Weighted Blanket instead.

Are weighted blankets safe?

+

Weighted blankets are generally safe for most adults and older children. However, they're not recommended for children under 40 lbs, people with respiratory conditions, poor circulation, or mobility issues that make it hard to remove the blanket. If you have any concerns, check with your doctor first. For younger children, consider our Huloo Sleep Kids Weighted Blanket which is specifically designed for kids.

How do I clean my weighted blanket?

+

The YnM blanket itself is machine-washable on a gentle cycle with cold water, and you can tumble dry on low heat. If you purchase the separate duvet cover, it's also machine-washable and makes cleaning even easier. For longevity, some people prefer spot-cleaning when possible.

Will this blanket make me too hot?

+

The YnM classic blanket works well for most people in standard room temperatures (65-75°F). However, if you tend to sleep hot or live in a warm climate without AC, you might find it too warm. In that case, we recommend our YnM Cooling Weighted Blanket which is specifically designed with bamboo rayon fabric for superior cooling.

Is this blanket suitable for children?

+

This adult-sized blanket is not recommended for children under 40 lbs due to safety concerns. We have a dedicated Huloo Sleep Kids Weighted Blanket that is specifically designed for children and teens with appropriate sizes, weights, and ultra-soft minky fabric.

Is this weighted blanket portable?

+

This YnM weighted blanket is designed primarily for home use. While you can transport it, its size (60"x80") and weight (15-20 lbs) make it less ideal for frequent travel. If you need a portable option for travel, office, or on-the-go use, check out our Wemore Lap Blanket which is specifically designed for portability (24"x29", 7 lbs).

Will a weighted blanket help with my insomnia?

+

Many people with insomnia find weighted blankets helpful for sleep. The deep pressure therapy promotes relaxation and can help quiet racing thoughts. That said, results vary from person to person. Weighted blankets work best as part of a good sleep routine—not as a standalone cure.

How long does it take to adjust to a weighted blanket?

+

Most people get used to it within 1-2 weeks. The first few nights might feel a bit strange or even slightly claustrophobic, but that usually fades as your body adapts. Try starting with shorter periods—like using it while reading or watching TV—before sleeping under it all night.
