Sizing Guide for Optimal Comfort

1

Calculate Your Ideal Weight

Take 10% of your body weight and add 1 pound. For ~140lbs adults, 15lbs is ideal. 80x87 inches for single use.

2

Choose the Right Size

80x87 inches for individual use. 88x104 inches for sharing. Measure your bed to ensure proper fit.

3

Adjustment Period

Allow 2-7 days to adjust. Start with 20-30 minutes nightly, gradually increasing use.

Pro Tip: Weighted blankets are designed to cover your body, not hang off the bed. Their smaller size is intentional for optimal pressure distribution.

How heavy should my weighted blanket be?

We recommend 10% of your body weight plus 1 pound. For most adults (130-160lbs), 15 lbs is ideal. If you're between sizes or prefer deeper pressure, size up.
