How to Use a Gua Sha Stone: Step-by-Step Guide

Gua sha is an ancient Chinese healing technique that involves scraping the skin with a smooth tool to improve circulation, reduce inflammation, and promote overall wellness. When used correctly, gua sha can provide numerous benefits for both your skin and body.

What You'll Need

  • A gua sha stone (jade, rose quartz, or obsidian work well)
  • Facial oil or serum
  • Clean skin

Step 1: Prepare Your Skin

Start with clean, dry skin. Apply a generous amount of facial oil or serum to the area you'll be treating. This helps the gua sha stone glide smoothly and prevents irritation.

Step 2: Basic Techniques

Hold the gua sha stone at a 15-45 degree angle. Always move in upward and outward motions, following the natural contours of your face and body.

Step 3: Facial Gua Sha Routine

  • Jawline: Start at the chin and move along the jawline towards the ears
  • Cheeks: From the nose, sweep outward towards the temples
  • Forehead: From the center, move outward towards the hairline
  • Neck: From the collarbone, move upward towards the chin

Benefits of Gua Sha

  • Reduces puffiness and swelling
  • Improves blood circulation
  • Stimulates lymphatic drainage
  • Relieves muscle tension
  • Enhances product absorption

Tips for Beginners

Start with gentle pressure and gradually increase as you become more comfortable. Remember to clean your gua sha stone after each use. For best results, use 2-3 times per week.
