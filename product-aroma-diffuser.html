Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the diffuser scent last?
With proper use, the 6.7oz fragrance oil typically lasts 60-90 days. Results may vary based on room size, temperature, and number of reeds used.
Does it come with reeds?
Yes! The set includes natural cotton diffuser sticks and preserved baby's breath flowers for an elegant look.
What scents are available?
This set features Sandalwood Rose scent. We also offer other fragrance options including Lavender, Vanilla, Ocean Breeze, and more.
Is this safe for pets?
While the oils are natural, keep diffusers out of reach of pets and ensure proper ventilation. If you have concerns, consult your veterinarian.
How do I adjust the scent intensity?
Use more reeds for stronger fragrance, fewer for a lighter scent. You can also flip the reeds periodically to refresh the aroma.
Can I use my own essential oils?
Yes, the glass bottle can be refilled with your preferred essential oils or fragrance oils.
