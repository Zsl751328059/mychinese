How to Use Your Reed Diffuser

Using your reed diffuser is simple and enjoyable. Follow these tips for the best experience:

How to Use:

  1. Unbox - Remove the diffuser bottle and reeds from packaging
  2. Prepare - Remove the seal and screw on the cap
  3. Insert Reeds - Place 2-4 fiber sticks into the bottle
  4. Flip & Enjoy - Flip reeds after 1 hour for faster diffusion

Pro Tips:

  1. Flip Regularly - Flip reeds every few days for consistent scent
  2. Adjust Intensity - Use more reeds for stronger scent, fewer for lighter
  3. Placement - Place near entryways or open areas for best diffusion
  4. Refill Available - Purchase refill oils when yours runs out
