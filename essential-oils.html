Essential Oils Buying Guide

Everything you need to know before buying essential oils

💧

Check Purity

Look for 100% pure essential oils without synthetic additives, fillers, or diluents.

🌿

Choose Therapeutic Grade

Therapeutic-grade oils are distilled at the right temperature and pressure to preserve beneficial compounds.

🏷️

Read Labels Carefully

Ensure labels list botanical names, country of origin, and extraction method.

🧪

Third-Party Testing

Look for oils that have been tested by independent labs for purity and quality.
