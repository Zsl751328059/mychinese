TL;DR (Quick Summary)

Yes, we earn money when you buy products through links on our site. But this doesn't change our reviews or recommendations. We only recommend products we genuinely believe in.

What Is Affiliate Marketing?

How Affiliate Links Work on Our Site

When you click on an affiliate link on Healthy Living and make a purchase:

  • A small cookie is placed on your device by the affiliate network
  • If you purchase within a certain time period (typically 24-48 hours), we earn a commission
  • The price you pay is the same whether or not you use our link
  • You don't pay anything extra for using our links

Our Commitment to Honest Reviews

We want to be completely transparent: our affiliate relationships do NOT influence our product reviews or recommendations. Here's our promise:

  • Editorial Independence: Our editorial team creates all content independently, without influence from our affiliate partners
  • Honest Assessments: We only recommend products we have tested, used, or thoroughly researched
  • No Pay-to-Play: We don't accept payment for reviews or guaranteed rankings
  • Reader First: We only recommend products we'd use ourselves or give to our families

Why We Use Affiliate Links

Affiliate marketing allows us to:

  • Keep our website free to use for everyone
  • Offset the costs of maintaining and improving our site
  • Compensate our team for researching and writing reviews
  • Continue providing free, valuable content to our readers

Products We Recommend
