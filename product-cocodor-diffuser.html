How long does the fragrance last?

+

The COCODOR Flower Diffuser typically lasts 4-6 weeks depending on the number of reeds used and room conditions. Turning the reeds upside down occasionally helps maintain consistent fragrance.

+

This diffuser is best suited for small to medium rooms. For larger spaces, consider using multiple diffusers or a larger size option.
