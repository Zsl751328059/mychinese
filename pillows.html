Pillow Buying Guide

Everything you need to know before buying a pillow

🛏️

Know Your Sleeping Position

Choose firmness based on how you sleep - soft for stomach, medium for back, firm for side sleepers.

🌡️

Consider Temperature

Look for cooling materials like gel-infused foam or breathable covers if you tend to sleep hot.

🧵

Material Matters

Memory foam for support, down for luxury, latex for durability - choose based on your needs.

🤒

Neck Pain Relief

Consider ergonomic or cervical pillows designed to maintain proper spinal alignment.

FAQs About Pillows

Most pillows should be replaced every 1-2 years. Signs it's time for a new pillow include lumps, flattened appearance, or waking up with neck pain.
