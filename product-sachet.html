The History Of Chinese Herbal Sachets

Herbal sachets have been an important part of Chinese culture for over 2,000 years. They were originally used during the Dragon Boat Festival to ward off evil spirits and bring good luck.

In traditional Chinese culture, different herbs are believed to have different properties. Lavender is associated with calm and relaxation, mugwort with protection and purification, and sandalwood with peace and tranquility.

Today, herbal sachets are still popular in China and around the world as a natural way to freshen the air and create a peaceful atmosphere. This traditional design and blend stays true to the ancient methods that have been used for centuries.
