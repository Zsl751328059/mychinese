Sleep Mask Buying Guide

Everything you need to know before buying a sleep mask

🎭

Light Blocking

Look for masks with contoured designs and adjustable nose bridges for 100% light blocking.

😴

Comfort & Pressure

Choose masks with deep eye cups to avoid pressure on eyes, especially for side sleepers.

🧵

Material Matters

Silk or satin for luxury, memory foam for structure, cooling fabrics for hot sleepers.

✈️

Travel-Friendly

Look for lightweight, compact designs with included travel pouches for on-the-go use.

FAQs About Sleep Masks

Yes! Sleep masks help create complete darkness, which signals to your brain that it's time to sleep. They can improve sleep quality by blocking light pollution from screens, streetlights, and other sources.

Look for masks with contoured designs and deep eye cups that won't press against your eyes or temples when sleeping on your side. Memory foam masks with tilt angles are especially good.

When used properly, sleep masks are safe. Avoid tight-fitting masks that press on your eyes. Look for masks with deep eye cups that don't touch your eyelids.

Most masks can be hand-washed with mild soap and water. Silk masks should be cleaned gently. Always check the care instructions for your specific mask.
