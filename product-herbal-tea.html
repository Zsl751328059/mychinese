How To Brew The Perfect Cup

Brewing this herbal tea correctly is important to get the full flavor and experience. Follow these simple steps for the best results:

  1. Heat water - Bring fresh, filtered water to a boil (212°F / 100°C)
  2. Place tea bag - Put one tea bag in your favorite mug
  3. Pour water - Pour 8-10 oz of boiling water over the tea bag
  4. Steep - Let it steep for 5-7 minutes for full flavor extraction
  5. Remove tea bag - Squeeze gently to release all the flavor
  6. Enjoy - Drink plain or add a touch of honey or lemon if desired
